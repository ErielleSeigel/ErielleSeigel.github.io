My name is Erielle Seigel. I am an aspiring Game Designer with a focus on 2D  character creation and concept design work. Growing up, I’ve always found myself fascinated by stories, especially ones that I myself could interact with. This, along with playing games with my father growing up, are what have lead me to developing an interest in creating my own games. It inspired me to try to create my own rich stories like those I got to experience with my father as I grew up.

---

When it comes to the creation of these games, I do not think about it in terms of code like my peers tend to around me. I think of it mostly through a design and storytelling standpoint. What is the world that I want to create or am being asked to create? What kind  of characters would fit within these worlds? What kind of personalities would the environment cultivate? Things like this are what are typically running through my brain when I am trying to create these characters for any game project. It is an incredibly fun endeavor and I love nothing more than being able to work within a team to see these creations and stories come to life. I also try to research about the world that we are taking inspiration from for our characters and stories so they can fit as well as possible. Some projects I like to highlight are Echo, which I created for the 2024 Cross Cultural Game Jam, as well as my senior project game titled Soul Sync. I was the lead character designer in both of these games, and they both taught me many things about working in a team and designing cohesively with the setting and gameplay of the games. Echo was a game with echolocation as a main component to gameplay and so I created a bat character. Soul Sync required a party of characters that matched a fantastical setting, so I got to work on creating a unique cast of fantasy and sci-fi creatures for it.

---

Working as a team to create these stories and then seeing characters and such come to life through them has been just as fulfilling as I had always dreamed as a kid. It has taught me how to think creatively on how to connects characters to the world and gameplay. As my passion grows, I aspire to continue to create, both worlds and characters within them.
